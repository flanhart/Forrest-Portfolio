About me

I am an Arizona State University graduate with a Bachelors of Science degree in Criminology and Criminal Justice. While I initially went to school for computer software engineering, a ride-along with my Uncle changed me mind. I decided I wanted to try a career in the criminal justice field. After spending the last five years in this field I have decided that I want to pursue my original passion in Computer Software Engineering.

My current position is Surveillance Officer with the Maricopa County Adult Probation Department. My responsibilites are to keep the community safe by supervising individuals who have been placed on probation by the Superior court. Working in this position has given me the flexibility I need to participate in this exciting bootcamp to develop the skills necessary to break into this great career.
